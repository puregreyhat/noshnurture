## Troubleshooting ğŸ”§

### Mic Doesn't Work
**Solution**: 
1. Check browser permission (allow microphone)
2. Try different browser (Chrome works best)
3. Refresh page and try again

### "I didn't understand that"
**Solution**: 
1. Rephrase more clearly
2. Ask about: expiry, recipes, inventory, or cuisine
3. Speak one sentence at a time

### No sound coming back
**Solution**:
1. Check device volume
2. Unmute by clicking speaker icon
3. Check browser audio permissions

### Keeps asking for microphone
**Solution**: 
1. Grant permission permanently (not just once)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try incognito/private mode
